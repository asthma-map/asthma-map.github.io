# Molecular Relations

The AsthmaMap Molecular Relations layer consists of cell-type-specific interconnected diagrams. The diagrams are automatically assembled based on the AsthmaMap Cellular Interactions and information from the MetaCore (licence purchased by the CNRS-EISBM within the eTRIKS project). Pathways for merging were prepared in the SBGN Activity Flow format (Le Nov√®re et al., 2009, [PMID 19668183](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/?term=19668183)) and adjusted to ensure compatibility for the pathway-merging pipeline, automatically merged for a specific cell type and automatically laid out in the yEd Graph Editor using the One-Click Layout.
